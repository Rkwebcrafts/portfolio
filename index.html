FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Got questions? I have got answers. If you do not see yours here, feel free to reach out.

A simple landing page takes 3–5 days. A full business website with up to 5 pages takes 7–10 days. Complex dashboards may take 2–3 weeks. I always give you a clear timeline in my proposal before we start.

Yes, absolutely. Once the project is complete and payment is settled, you own 100% of the code. I will also provide a simple guide so you or your team can make basic text and image updates without needing a developer.

Minor text or image changes within the first 2 weeks are free. After that, you can opt for my monthly maintenance plan (₹2,000/month) which includes up to 5 content updates per month. One-off changes are charged at ₹300 each.

I do not provide hosting or domains directly, but I will guide you through buying them. I recommend Hostinger or Namecheap for domains, and Netlify or Vercel for free hosting. I will also deploy your site for free on these platforms as part of the project.

I accept UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and PayPal for international clients. For projects above ₹5,000, I take 50% advance before starting and 50% on delivery. For smaller projects, full payment upfront.

Currently, I specialize in frontend development — product pages, carts, and checkout UIs. For full e-commerce with payment gateways (Razorpay, Stripe), I can build the frontend and connect you with a backend developer, or we can use no-code tools like Shopify.
